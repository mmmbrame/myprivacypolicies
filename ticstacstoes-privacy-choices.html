User Privacy Choices for Tics Tacs & Toes

You control how much data is shared when you use this app.

1. Local vs Online

Local play Solo and pass-and-play games on one device do not require sending your gameplay to our servers. Enjoy full privacy for those modes.
Online multiplayer If you create or join an online room, minimal technical and gameplay data is processed so matches stay in sync. You do not need to sign in with an account.

2. Room codes

Only people who know your room code can join that match. Do not share the code if you want to keep the game private.

3. No account required

You are not required to register, provide an email, or connect a social login to play.

4. What online play uses

For online games, typical data includes an anonymous device/session identifier, room code, and live game state (board, turns, scores). This is used only to run the game—not for targeted ads sold by us.

5. Questions

For privacy questions or requests, use the App Store contact options or see our full Privacy Policy.
