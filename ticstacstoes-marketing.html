Tics Tacs & Toes

Classic Tic Tac Toe on a small board—or huge multi-grid battles with strikes, CPUs, and online rooms.

Play your way

  • 3×3 — Fast classic matches against a friend or the CPU.
  • Big boards — 6×6 and up: rack up strikes across many mini-grids.
  • Modes — Player vs player, you vs CPU, and three-player setups on large grids.
  • Online — Create or join a room with a code and play in real time.
