Privacy Policy for Speedy Knowledge

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Speedy Knowledge is a multiplayer word game. This policy describes how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

To provide multiplayer gameplay, we collect:

  • Display name — The name you enter when creating or joining a game (e.g., "Player 1"). This is visible to other players in the same room.
  • Anonymous identifiers — Firebase Authentication may assign an anonymous ID to enable real-time sync. This is not linked to your identity.

How We Use Your Information

Your display name and game answers are used solely to run the game (showing who's in the room, scoring, etc.). We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing.

Data Storage

Game data (room codes, player names, answers, scores) is stored temporarily on Firebase (Google) servers for the duration of the game session. Room data may be retained briefly after a game ends.

Third-Party Services

We use Firebase (Google) for authentication and real-time database. Their privacy policy applies: Firebase Privacy.

Children

Speedy Knowledge is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 beyond what is necessary for gameplay.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent changes.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the App Store listing or your preferred method.
